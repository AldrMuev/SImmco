About us

SIMMCO Inc. is a trusted provider of HVAC cleaning and sanitization services, offering high-quality solutions to both residential and commercial clients across Canada. With a wide range of services including duct cleaning, HVAC maintenance, dry ice blasting, and disinfecting, we ensure that our clients' systems are operating at peak efficiency and that their environments are safe and clean.

We have built a reputation for excellence by partnering with leading brands and employing the latest technologies in the industry. Our fully trained and certified technicians are committed to delivering top-notch service, guaranteeing the smooth and effective completion of every project, from routine maintenance to specialized cleaning services.
