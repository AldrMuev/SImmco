About Us

SIMMCO Inc. is a trusted provider of HVAC cleaning and sanitization services, offering tailored solutions for residential and commercial clients across Ontario. Our services include duct cleaning, HVAC maintenance, dry ice blasting, and disinfecting, designed to meet the highest standards of quality and efficiency.

We ensure the smooth operation of HVAC systems by utilizing advanced technologies and employing trained technicians to handle projects from start to finish. Whether you need routine cleaning or specialized sanitization, SIMMCO delivers reliable results that improve air quality and system performance.

Our offerings are flexible, catering to the specific needs of each client to ensure a healthy and efficient indoor environment. In addition to providing top-tier services, we also focus on maintaining environmental and workplace safety. Our processes adhere to strict health and safety standards, ensuring minimal impact on people and the environment.

With a commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction, SIMMCO continues to build long-lasting relationships while delivering high-quality HVAC solutions you can trust.
